My name is Pol and I am an incoming Machine Learning PhD student at [ LIONS - EPFL](https://www.epfl.ch/labs/lions/).

My current focus is on Machine Learning for Combinatorial domains such as Integer Linear Programming (ILPs), scheduling and graphs.

In the past I did some Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) + Scheduling + Hardware Accelerators research [@GeorgiaTech](https://ece.gatech.edu/) and [@UPC](https://bnn.upc.edu/), some Large Language Models (LLMs) [@Capchase](https://www.capchase.com/), end-to-end ML forecasting and pricing optimization [@MANGO](https://www.mango.com/), self driving cars stuff [@BCNeMotorsport](https://bcnemotorsport.upc.edu/home/), computer vision [@TAVIL](https://tavil.com/) and organized Spain's first and largest Datathons [@AED](https://aed.cat/).
